About Me

A dedicated peer who understands exactly what it takes to succeed at Carlmont High School.

Anay Shah

Founder & Lead Tutor

Hi, I’m Anay! Having successfully conquered Carlmont's rigorous freshman tracks—including Advanced Standing English 1 and advanced math—I know precisely what it takes to thrive here. I started Peninsula Prep with a simple goal: to provide incoming Carlmont students with the targeted academic support that helped me succeed, while giving back to our local community.

As a solo founder, I am personally invested in every student's success. I bring my direct experience with the curriculum to every session, ensuring that my tutoring isn't just generic advice, but a focused roadmap tailored for the exact classes you will take.
