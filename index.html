What I Offer

Elementary / Middle

Building foundational skills in math, reading, and writing to prepare younger students for long-term academic success in high school.

High School Core

Targeted support in subjects like Algebra 2/Trigonometry, Geometry, and Advanced Standing English 1, taught by a peer who just mastered the curriculum.

STEM & Research Focus

Specialized tutoring in Chemistry and independent scientific research methodology for students looking to push their boundaries.

How It Works

Donation-Based Model

Quality tutoring shouldn't be a luxury. Instead of a fixed pricing model, I accept suggested tips via cash, Zelle, or gift cards following our sessions.

Community Impact

All proceeds collected act as donations directly to Samaritan House to purchase books and educational resources for families in the Bay Area.
