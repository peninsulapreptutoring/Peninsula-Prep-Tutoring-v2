About Me

Hi, I'm Anay Shah.

I am a student with a deep interest in STEM, complex problem-solving, and independent scientific research. Having recently completed rigorous coursework including Algebra 2/Trigonometry and Advanced Standing English 1, the syllabus you or your student are tackling is completely fresh in my mind.

Whether I'm helping a middle schooler build a foundation or guiding a high school peer through advanced coursework, I focus on breaking down concepts into manageable steps. My approach is rooted in genuine understanding rather than rote memorization.

Outside of the classroom, I am a competitive water polo athlete. Balancing athletics with heavy academics has taught me the discipline, resilience, and time-management skills that I bring to every tutoring session.
