About Me

Hi, I'm Anay Shah.

I enjoy tackling difficult questions that require persistence and creative thinking. As a student deeply interested in chemistry and scientific research, I love uncovering how different STEM concepts connect across various fields.

In the classroom, I am known for taking my academics seriously. I focus on truly understanding the material rather than simply memorizing it, asking questions to deepen my knowledge, and approaching complex problems with determination. I also believe in pushing my boundaries outside the standard high school curriculum by exploring concurrent enrollment at community colleges and actively seeking STEM enrichment programs.

Outside the Classroom

Future Goals

Looking ahead, I plan to continue taking challenging courses, participating in science research competitions, and developing the skills necessary to excel in collegiate STEM fields.
