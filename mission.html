My Mission

Peninsula Prep was built on a core belief: Quality academic support shouldn't be a luxury, and community action starts locally.

Bridging the Academic Gap

Transitioning to high school is notoriously difficult. Carlmont's rigorous pacing catches many freshmen off guard. While commercial tutoring agencies charge exorbitant hourly fees, they often lack the hyper-specific knowledge required to excel in specific classrooms. I built Peninsula Prep to fill that gap. As a recent graduate of these exact courses, I offer insider knowledge on teacher expectations, curriculum nuances, and effective study habits that a textbook cannot teach.

Supporting Samaritan House

I do not charge an hourly fee. I run entirely on a suggested tip-based model ($20-$25 per session). 100% of the profits generated are used to purchase educational books, which are then independently donated to Samaritan House to support low-income families in the Bay Area.

Why Tip-Based?

I operate on trust. The tip-based model ensures that students who need help can get it, regardless of their family's immediate financial situation. It also allows families who value the service to contribute directly to a vital local charity. When you book a session with me, you aren't just investing in your own academic future; you are putting books directly into the hands of children in our community who need them most.
