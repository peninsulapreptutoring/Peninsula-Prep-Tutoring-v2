My Mission

Education is the most powerful tool for growth, yet quality academic support is often gated by high costs. Peninsula Prep was founded to make excellent, personalized tutoring accessible to students across all grade levels—from elementary school to high school.

The Tip-Based Model

To ensure this service remains accessible, Peninsula Prep operates entirely as a solo project on a donation-based mission. Instead of requiring a fixed pricing model, I collect suggested tips for my time.

Payments are easily accepted via cash, Zelle, or gift cards.

100% of all proceeds collected act as donations to Samaritan House to purchase books and educational resources for families in need.

*Please note: Peninsula Prep is an independent, student-run initiative and is not formally partnered with Samaritan House. They simply serve as the recipient of the funds we raise together.
